The Best GD Banana Bread

  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 cup of shortening
  • 2 eggs (or 3 mashed bananas to subsitute)
  • 3 mashed bananas
  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 1 tsp of baking soda
  • 2 tsp of milk (or buttermilk)
  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees
  2. Cream together shortening, & sugar. Add in eggs, milk, & bananas.
  3. Mix salt, baking soda, & flour in a separate bowl. Add slowly to the banana mixture.
  4. Grease a bread pan and bake for 1 hour at 350 degrees.
  5. Enjoy!

Killer Cookies'n'Cream Cookies
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 14.2 oz oreo pudding mix
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 2 1/4 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup white chocolate chips
  • 15 oreos, crushed coarsely
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Cream together butter & sugars. Add pudding mix until blended. Add in eggs & vanilla, mixing well.
  3. In a separate bowl mix flour and baking soda. Add in gradually to pudding mixture until fully combined.
  4. Stir in chocolate chips & oreo chunks.
  5. Drop cookies by rounded spoonfuls on to a greased cookie sheet.
  6. Bake for 10-12 minutes at 350 degrees.
  7. Enjoy!

B-Wordin' Brownies
  • 1 1/2 cups of flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2 (12 ounce) bags semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 4 eggs
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Combine flour, baking soda, and salt in a small bowl & set aside.
  3. Combine sugar, water, and butter in a saucepan. Stir over medium heat until the butter melts & sugar dissolves.
  4. Remove from heat, then stir in one bag of chocolate chips & vanilla extract until chocolate has melted. Cool for 5 minutes, then pour into mixing bowl, & beat in eggs one at a time until smooth.
  5. Fold in flour mixture until incorporated, then fold n remaining bad of chocolate chips.
  6. Grease a 9x13 pan, and pour in brownie mixture.
  7. Bake at 350 degrees for 45-55 minutes. Top should be dry, and edges should start to pull away from pan.
  8. Cool completely, cut and enjoy!
